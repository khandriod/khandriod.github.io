About Me

Hi, I am pleased to introduce myself to you. My name is Jabbar Shah and I am from Pakistan. Currently living in Seoul. I am graduated with a master’s degree from the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Hanyang University, Seoul, South Korea.

I have a master’s degree specializing in Advance Infrastructure Systems from the lab of Automation and Construction Management. During my enrollment at Hanyang university, I was involved in a couple of lab projects that involved project management for the Intelligent Excavation System for earthmoving projects and AR and VR applications. I have a 4-year bachelor’s degree in civil engineering from Pakistan.

I have good knowledge of programming (Python) and data analytics. I have been extensively taking courses on machine learning and deep learning technologies. Currently, I am working on computer vision applications for object detection, segmentation and object tracking. I am very confident in using the deep learning framework (Pytorch, TensorFlow) to construction automation advantages.

In my free time, I will most probably be travelling, reading books, or having coffee in cozy cafe.
